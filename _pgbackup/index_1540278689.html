i just want to go home

Imagine, you are on your way home after a long and hard day of work. Pettersson from Kaiserslautern, Germany has the soundtrack for this time. With the reduced lofi beats on his new album 'I Just Want To Go Home' you can forget all your problems for a short time.

i don't know where this 

The german producer from cologne named pettersson has this special kind of magic you can't explain. His works till yet were all different in style and work  on his fourth release he combined everything in a logic way. If he goes 'mit schwung in den tag' and uses sounds from his beloved cheap guitar combined with his addiction to sleep or if he's stating the opposite with 'zu viel schlaf macht müde' starts the day with heavy but slow base and ends up in 'the opposite of relaxing'.

i like my cheap guitar

Here you go: pettersson's third work. It's just amazing to follow his development throughout his releases. Every release is a hidden treasure to fill days with the joy of discovery. Whether he did classic lofi beats in the past (i want you here with me) or tried some housy tunes (i am addicted to sleep), he was always expressing a lot of emotions. This time he mixes all the skills and emotions he made in the past and combines it with a cheap guitar that you will like too!

i am addicted to sleep

pettersson is addicted to sleep and it sounds like he's addicted to some of the lofi heroes, too. Even if he states ''This beat makes me tired'' its lovely alive and the opposite of boring. When he changes his style after this song into a lofi-ish house groove you know that there's someone deeply into it.

Okay to be honest - there is good music and there's excellent music. With chopped sounds, slow loops and love for the detail, pettersson puts his beats together and develops them into highly addictive pieces of music.
